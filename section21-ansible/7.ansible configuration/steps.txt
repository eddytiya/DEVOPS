ORDER OF ANSIBLE CONFIG
1. ANSIBLE_CONFIG(enviromnet varaible if set)
2.ansible.cfg(in the current dir)
3.~/.ansible.cfg(in the home dir)
4./etc/ansible/ansible.cfg
